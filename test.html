About Me

Hi! My name is Rachel. I’m a current MSI-LIS graduate student at Drexel University’s College of Computing and Informatics. I started working in libraries as a teen and most recently worked as a public library programs and public relations assistant before pursuing my Masters in Library and Information Science. I have a demonstrated history of planning innovative library programs including film screenings, concerts, lectures, author talks and other educational events in libraries.

Some of my hobbies include baking, geocaching and listening to music. Check out the photo gallery and playlist below to learn more and follow @tinyskillets on Instagram to follow the culinary adventures of my trusty 6 inch cast iron skillet.
